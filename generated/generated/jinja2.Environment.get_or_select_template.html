1.1.3.6.1.10. jinja2.Environment.get_or_select_template¶

Environment.get_or_select_template(template_name_or_list, parent=None, globals=None)[source]¶

Does a typecheck and dispatches to select_template() if an iterable of template names is given, otherwise to get_template().

New in version 2.3.
